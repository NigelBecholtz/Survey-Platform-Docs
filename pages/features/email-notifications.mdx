# Email Notifications

Per-survey email: admin alerts when a survey is completed, and optional respondent summary in their language.

## Admin notification (survey completed)

- **When**: Right after a survey is submitted (response created).
- **Who**: Addresses configured in **Survey → Email notifications → Alert email recipients**.
- **Content**: Survey title, response ID, completed at, respondent name/email (if collected), total score, link to view response in dashboard.
- **SMTP**: Uses the **per-survey SMTP** (host, port, encryption, username, password) and **From address / From name** set in that survey’s email settings. No dependency on `.env` MAIL_* for this flow when survey settings are present.

## Respondent summary email

- **When**: After submit, only if the respondent entered an **email** in the survey.
- **Content**: Survey title, thank-you message, list of questions and their answers in the **language the respondent chose**. Optional total score. Plain-text and HTML parts.
- **SMTP**: Same per-survey SMTP when configured; otherwise falls back to Laravel default mailer.
- **Language**: The `language` sent in the submit payload is used to translate question/option text and email UI strings (from `lang/*.json`).

## Per-survey email settings (dashboard)

- **SMTP**: host, port, encryption (TLS/SSL/None), username, password (stored encrypted).
- **From address** and **From name** (e.g. `noreply@example.com`, “My Survey”).
- **Alert recipients**: list of emails that receive the “survey completed” notification.
- **Status**: Active / inactive. When inactive, no admin or respondent emails are sent.

## Deliverability (avoiding spam)

- Emails are sent **multipart** (HTML + plain text) and with **Reply-To** set to the From address when configured.
- Spam is usually caused by **DNS (SPF/DKIM)** for the From domain not authorizing the SMTP server. The dashboard shows a short “Emails going to spam?” tip; see the project’s `docs/email-deliverability.md` for full guidance (SPF, DKIM, DMARC, transactional providers).
