# Surveys

Create, configure, and publish surveys with multiple question types, pages, and optional scoring.

## Survey model

- **Title / description** – Translatable (JSON: e.g. `en`, `nl`, `de`).
- **Goal** – e.g. lead qualification (used for analytics/filtering).
- **Status** – draft, published, etc.
- **Public link** – slug for `/public/:slug` (generated on publish or manually).
- **Default language** – e.g. `en`.
- **Contact form** – `collect_name`, `collect_email`; `contact_form_position`: before questions or after questions (before advice).
- **Theme** – optional (primary color, background, font, logo).

## Question types

- **Single choice** – one option.
- **Multiple choice** – multiple options.
- **Open text** – free text.
- **Scale** – numeric scale (min/max, optional labels).

Questions can be **required**, have **order** and **page_number**, and **weight** for scoring. Answer options can have a **score** and optional **audience_impact** (JSON).

## Score ranges and advice

Surveys can have **score ranges** (min/max score) with **advice type** and **advice text** (translatable). After submission, the total score is computed and the matching range’s advice is shown to the respondent and stored on the response.

## Builder (dashboard)

- Add/edit/delete questions and options; reorder; paginate.
- Configure score ranges and advice.
- Set contact form position and default language.
- Link to **Email notifications** (per-survey SMTP and recipients).
- Design: theme, logo upload.
- Publish (generates/uses public slug), unpublish, duplicate.

## Public access

- **By slug**: `/public/:slug` – anyone with the link.
- **By token**: `/s/:token` – audience link; can pre-select audience and optionally restrict to one audience.

Both flows start with a **language** step, then optional contact/audience step(s), then questions, then advice and thank-you.
