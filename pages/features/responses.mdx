# Responses

Submitted survey data: answers, score, advice, and metadata.

## Response model

- **Survey**, **company**, optional **audience**.
- **Name**, **email** (if contact form collected them).
- **Total score** (if survey has scoring).
- **Advice type** and **advice text** (from the matching score range).
- **Status** (e.g. new, read).
- **Response answers** – one per answered question: `question_id`, `answer_text` (open text), `answer_option_id` (choice), `score` (if applicable).

## Dashboard

- **Response list** – filter by survey, date, audience, search; export CSV/Excel.
- **Response detail** – all answers with question/option text (translated), score, advice, respondent name/email, timestamps. Optionally update status.

## Export

- Export filtered responses as CSV or Excel (XLSX) with columns for survey, respondent, answers, score, advice, etc.

## After submit

- Response is created and linked to survey (and optional audience).
- Admin notification email can be sent (if survey email settings are configured).
- If the respondent gave an email, a **respondent summary email** is sent (answers in their chosen language).
